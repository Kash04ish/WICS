Pradhan Mantri Mudra Yojana (PMMY)
Ministry of Finance, Govt of India
Loan
Collateral-free loans for small business owners. Three categories — Shishu (up to ₹50,000), Kishore (₹50,000–5L), and Tarun (₹5–10L). Ideal for women starting home-based or micro businesses.
Loan:Up to ₹10 Lakh
Collateral:None
Bank:Any scheduled bank
Mahila Udyam Nidhi Scheme
Punjab National Bank / SIDBI
Loan
Loans up to ₹10 lakh for women entrepreneurs to set up or modernise small-scale industries. Soft terms, low interest rates, and relaxed repayment schedules specifically for women.
Loan:Up to ₹10 Lakh
Interest:Subsidised rate
For:Women entrepreneurs
Stand Up India
SIDBI / Ministry of Finance
Business
Bank loans between ₹10 lakh and ₹1 crore for SC/ST and women entrepreneurs to set up greenfield enterprises in manufacturing, services or trading sector.
Loan:₹10L – ₹1 Crore
Eligibility:Women / SC/ST
Type:Greenfield project
PM Kaushal Vikas Yojana (PMKVY)
Ministry of Skill Development, Govt of India
Skill Training
Free short-term skill training in 300+ job roles. Certification recognised nationally. Training centres across India with special focus on women and youth. Get a ₹8,000 reward on certification.
Cost:Free
Reward:₹8,000 on pass
Courses:300+ skill areas
Nai Roshni — Leadership for Minority Women
Ministry of Minority Affairs
Skill Training
Leadership development training for minority women to empower them to interact with government systems. Covers health, education, sanitation, and financial literacy. Free residential camps across India.
Cost:Free
Duration:3–6 days
Focus:Leadership & Literacy
Janani Suraksha Yojana (JSY)
Ministry of Health & Family Welfare
Health
Cash assistance to pregnant women for institutional delivery to reduce maternal and neonatal mortality. ₹1,400 in rural areas, ₹1,000 in urban areas. Available through ANM / ASHA workers in all states.
Benefit:₹1,000–₹1,400
For:Pregnant women
Through:ASHA / ANM worker
